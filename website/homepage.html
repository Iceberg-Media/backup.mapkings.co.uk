CHOOSE YOUR STAGES


From a stealthy Stage 1 daily-driver map to a built-engine Stage 3 monster, we calibrate to your goals.

STAGE 1

SOFTWARE-ONLY REMAP
PRICE: £ 180

Pure software calibration on a standard engine. No hardware changes. Typically +20-40% power and torque gains. Perfect for daily-driven cars.

  • No hardware required
  • +20-40% power gains
  • Reversible
  • Daily-drive friendly

STAGE 2

BOLT-ON MODIFICATIONS
PRICE: £ 200

Remap calibrated for cars with supporting hardware – downpipe, intercooler, intake or decat. Bigger, more aggressive gains.

  • Supporting hardware
  • +40-60% power gains
  • Requires premium fuel
  • Track-day ready

STAGE 3

BUILT ENGINE PACKAGE
PRICE: POA

Full-built performance spec: hybrid turbos, upgraded fuelling, internals. The map is the soul of a fully-built machine.

  • TCU Gearbox
  • Built engine spec
  • +60-100% + power gains
  • Forged internals
  • Race-grade calibration
